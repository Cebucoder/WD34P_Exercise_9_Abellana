ABOUT US

SACHI

Authentic japanese ramen okonomiyaki

The restaurant’s name is called “Sachi” which means “happy” or “happiness” in Japanese. This is also the name of Raul de Guzman’s daughter, who passed away when she was only 11 months old. Raul de Guzman and Kenneth Go are Filipino entrepreneurs who established Sachi in 2014.

Raul de Guzman wanted to open a ramen restaurant after having a delicious ramen bowl in Manila with his family. He fell deeper in love with Japan after studying and working there, and after witnessing a teppanyaki performance in Dubai.
